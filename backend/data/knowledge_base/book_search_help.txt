Book Search and Discovery on Bookify

Bookify offers multiple ways to discover books:

1. Search by Title: Enter the exact or partial book title
2. Search by Author: Find all books by your favorite authors
3. Search by Genre: Browse books in specific categories
4. Community Recommendations: See what other readers are enjoying
5. Rating-based Discovery: Find highly-rated books

Advanced Search Tips:
- Use quotes for exact phrases
- Combine multiple search terms
- Filter by publication year or rating
